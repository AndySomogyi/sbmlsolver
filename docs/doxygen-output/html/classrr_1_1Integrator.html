Fix tolerances for SBML tests.

Author
JKM, WBC, ETS, MTK In order to ensure that the results of the SBML test suite remain valid, this method enforces a lower bound on tolerance values. Sets minimum absolute and relative tolerances to Config::CVODE_MIN_ABSOLUTE and Config::CVODE_MIN_RELATIVE resp.
